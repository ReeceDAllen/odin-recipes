Baked Ziti

Description

This is a delicious baked ziti recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 1lb dry ziti pasta
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1lb lean ground beef
  • 2 26 ounce jars spaghetti sauce
  • 6 ounces sliced provolone cheese
  • 1.5 cups sour cream
  • 6 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tablespoons grated parmesan

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of lightly salted water to boil, add pasta, cookl until al dente, approx 8 minutes, drain.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, brown the ground beef and onion in a large skillet over medium heat. Stir in spaghetti sauce and simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Preheat the oven to 175C. Butter a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  4. Spread 1/2 of the pasta in the bottom of the dish, add provolone cheese, sour cream, & half of the meat sauce. Add the remaining ziti, the mozzarella cheese, and the remaining sauce. Top with grated parmesan cheese.
  5. Bke in the oven until heated through and cheese have melted, approximately 30 minutes.
