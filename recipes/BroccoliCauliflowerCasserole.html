Broccoli Cauliflower Casserole

Description

This is a tasty and healthy Broccoli Cauliflower Casserole.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups broccoli
  • 2 cups cauliflower
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 15 ounces crushed seasoned croutons

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 175C
  2. Place broccoli & cauliflower in separate saucepans, cover with water and boil, cook until tender (3-4 mins). Drain, and add to an 11x7 inch baking dish.
  3. Beat eggs until creamy, stir in 1/2 cup cheddar cheese, the mayonnaise, and onion, then pour over vegetables. Pour the butter on top, sprinkle with remaining cheddar, and scatter the croutons evenly.
  4. Bake until brown and bubbling, approximately 40 minutes.
    5. ur to 1 hour 15 minutes. Cool for 10 to 15 minutes before serving.
