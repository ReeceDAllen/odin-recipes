Mamaw's Chicken and Rice Casserole

Description

This is an old fashioned recipe for how to make Chicken & Rice Casserole from the good old days.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Breasts
    • Three boneless skinless chicken breasts cut to rough cubes.
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 cups instant White Rice
  • 1 can each of Cream of Chicken, Celery, and mushroom soup
  • salt, pepper, and other seasonings to taste
  • 1/2 cup butter sliced to pats

Steps

  1. Gather and prepare all ingredients.
  2. Preheat oven to 200C, grease sides and bottom of a casserole dish.
  3. Stir together chicken, water, rice, and soups in prepped dish. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Distribute butter evenly over top.
  5. Bake until rice is tender and chicken is cooked through. 1 hour to 1 hour 15 minutes. Cool for 10 to 15 minutes before serving.
